About Me

Quinn is a Bristol based Games Developer. She studied Games Technology at the University of the West of England and graduated at the top of her year. She was a 2021 TIGA graduate of the year.

Her favorite things to code are Pathfinding solutions and NPC systems. Quinn is extremely passionate about programming and puts her all into any task.

She engages in a range of nerdy hobbies: Reading, Dungeons & Dragons, and Miniature Painting to name a few. When it comes to video games Quinn plays all sorts, though she has a soft spot for Minecraft, Cyberpunk 2077, and League of Legends.
